In this Retail Installment Sale Agreement (“Agreement”), the words, “you” and “your” mean the Buyer and Co-signer, individually and collectively, who sign this Agreement. The words “Seller,” “we” and “us” mean the Seller named at the top of this Agreement. This Agreement incorporates by reference the Private Education Loan Final Disclosure Statement (the “Final Disclosure Statement”) that accompanies this Agreement.

A.  GENERAL TERMS

You agree to purchase from the Seller, on a time basis, subject to the terms of this Agreement, educational services (“Services”) offered by the Seller. You have been given the opportunity to purchase the Services for cash or to finance them pursuant to this Agreement. You have agreed to finance the purchase of the Services over time.

The Total of Payments shown on the Final Disclosure Statement is the total price of the Services if you pay for them over time so it includes finance charges. The Total of Payments shown in the Final Disclosure Statement assumes that all payments will be made as scheduled. The actual amount you will pay may be more or less depending on your payment record.

You acknowledge and agree that you may purchase additional Services from the Seller at a later date. Any such additional purchases of Services will not be added to the balance due under this Agreement and instead will be due and payable, or financed in a separate agreement, when you purchase them.

B.  PROMISE TO PAY

You promise to pay the Seller, in accordance with the terms of this Agreement and the payment schedule included in the Final Disclosure Statement the Total Loan Amount shown on the Final Disclosure Statement plus finance charges accruing on a daily simple interest basis on the unpaid balance at the Interest Rate shown on the Final Disclosure Statement from today’s date until this Agreement is paid in full, and any other charges or fees you owe according to the terms and conditions of this Agreement. Buyer and any Co-signer who sign this Agreement agree to be jointly and individually liable to us for payment of all amounts due under the Agreement, regardless of who receives the Services.

C.  LATE FEE; DISHONORED CHECK FEES

If you do not make a scheduled payment in full by its due date, you agree to pay the late fee shown in the Final Disclosure Statement. If any payment that you make on this Agreement is returned for any reason, you agree to pay the dishonored check fee shown in the Final Disclosure Statement.

D.  PAYMENT METHODS

You may make the payments due on this Agreement via check, money order, automated clearing house (ACH) debit from a checking or savings account, or major debit cards.

E.  RIGHT TO PREPAY

You have the right to prepay all or any part of the outstanding balance of this Agreement at any time without penalty.

F.  DEFAULT

Except as provided below, we may declare this Agreement to be in default if you have outstanding an amount exceeding one full payment for more than 10 days after the scheduled or deferred due dates, or you fail to pay the first payment or the last payment within 40 days of its scheduled or deferred due date.

G.  REMEDIES

At any time at least 15 days after you are in default, we may require you to immediately pay us, subject to any refund or notice requirement provided by applicable law, the remaining unpaid balance of the Total Loan Amount, finance charges and all other charges as provided for in this Agreement. If you default, we may refer your Agreement for collection by a third-party collection agency, initiate actions to collect all amounts owed, and/or exercise any other remedies available under law and this Agreement. Buyer and any Co-signer who sign this Agreement understand and agree that our rights and remedies under the Agreement are cumulative. Each of you agrees that we may pursue our rights and remedies against any of you individually, or against both of you collectively, in any order or combination we choose, except to the extent prohibited by law.

H.  COLLECTION FEES

If this Agreement is in default and referred to a collection agency or an outside attorney to collect the outstanding debt, you will pay the costs of collection, including reasonable attorneys’ fees, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

I.  WAIVER

The Seller will not lose any rights under this Agreement if it delays taking action for any reason. Any change to this Agreement must be in writing and signed by the Seller.

J.  ASSIGNMENT

You may not assign this Agreement or any of its benefits and obligations. The Seller may assign this Agreement at any time.

K.  APPLICABLE LAW

The Agreement is governed by and will be subject to applicable federal law and the law of the state of your permanent residence as shown on the front of this Agreement.

L.  CREDIT REPORTING

The Seller may report information about this Agreement to consumer credit bureaus. Late payments, missed payments or other defaults on your Agreement may be reflected on your credit report. You have the right to dispute the accuracy and completeness of any information reported about this Agreement to a consumer reporting agency. You may do this by giving us notice at our address shown on the front of this Agreement, and also by notifying the consumer reporting agency, as provided by law.

M.   OBLIGATION AND ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF BUYER

You agree to make all scheduled payments, in the amounts and by the due dates shown in the Final Disclosure Statement. You acknowledge that under the terms of this Agreement, the Services provided to the Buyer are provided as an ongoing and continuing service. You agree that the Buyer’s access to courses through the Seller is contingent upon this Agreement being paid according to its terms and that, in addition to the above remedies for default, if you fail to make any scheduled payment, to the extent permitted by law, the Seller may deny access to your course, withhold your certificate, or any other Seller service.

N.  MODIFICATIONS

This Agreement contains all of the terms of your agreement with us related to the subject matter of this Agreement. Any change to this Agreement must be in writing and we must sign it. No oral changes are binding.

O.  ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

This Agreement is not effective until accepted by the Seller. The Buyer and the Co-signer agree to notify the Seller of any change in their name or address, or the Buyer’s Seller enrollment status, within ten (10) days of such occurrence. You represent that you have not made any false written statement with respect to this Agreement. We do not intend to charge or collect, and you do not agree to pay, any finance charge that is more than the maximum amount permitted for this sale by applicable law. If you pay a finance charge or fee that is more than allowed by applicable law, we will apply it first to reduce the principal balance and, when the principal has been paid in full, refund it to you. You understand and agree that, for purposes of evaluating, reviewing, servicing, or collecting this Agreement, we may: (1) obtain and use credit information and other information about you provided by third parties and consumer reporting agencies; and (2) provide information about you and the status of this Agreement to third parties and consumer reporting agencies. We will, upon request, inform you whether we obtained a consumer report about you and, if we did, inform you of the name and address of the reporting agency that provided the consumer report. You also authorize us to contact the person identified by the Buyer as having authority to act on behalf of the Buyer in the event of the Buyer’s death.

P.  SEVERABILITY

If any provision of this Agreement is determined to be unenforceable or is prohibited by law, such provision shall be considered ineffective without invalidating the remaining provisions of this Agreement.

Q.  CONFLICT BETWEEN THE FINAL DISCLOSURE STATEMENT AND THIS AGREEMENT

You understand and agree that if the information in your Final Disclosure Statement conflicts with the information in this Agreement, the information in your Final Disclosure Statement shall control.

R.  COMMUNICATION CONSENT

You agree that we may monitor and record telephone calls regarding your account to assure the quality of our service or for other reasons. You agree that we may call you, using an automatic telephone dialing system or otherwise, leave you a voice, prerecorded, or artificial voice message, or send you a text, email, or other electronic message for any purpose related to the servicing and collection of your account with us (each a "Communication"). You agree that we may send a Communication to any telephone numbers, including cellular telephone numbers, or e-mail addresses you provided to us in connection with the origination of this Agreement or at any time in the future. You acknowledge and confirm that you have the authority to provide the consent because you are either the subscriber of the telephone number(s) or you are the non-subscriber customary user who has authority to provide the consent. You also agree that we may include your personal information in a Communication. You agree that we will not charge you for a Communication, but your service provider may. In addition, you understand and agree that we may always communicate with you in in any manner permissible by law that does not require your prior consent.

S.  CO-SIGNER RELEASE

Notwithstanding any other term of this Agreement, in the event that the Buyer dies before the balance due under this Agreement is paid in full, we will cancel the remaining balance due under this Agreement. In that event, Co-signer will be released from all liability to us under this Agreement. We will notify the Co-signer within a reasonable timeframe if this provision of this Agreement applies.
